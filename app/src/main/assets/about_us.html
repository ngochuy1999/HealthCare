SECURITY & DATA
SnapShop’s checkout is powered by Stripe, an industry leader also used by companies like Deliveroo, Lyst and Asos. Stripe uses the highest levels of security and anti-fraud technology to keep your details secure. Your contact and shipping address is sent directly to the merchants who will ship out your items and provide you with tracking details.
